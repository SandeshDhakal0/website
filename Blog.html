Technology in Health care

As health care becomes more automated, doctors are increasingly using robots to perform various tasks,
such as diagnosing illness and performing surgery. With healthcare costs rising at a staggering rate, the
industry is looking for new ways to improve efficiency and quality. Healthtech is helping make this possible.
Through the use of technology, hospitals can improve efficiency and expand access to healthcare. This is exactly
what healthcare innovators are doing.

Technology in Entertainment

As technology has changed the way consumers watch television, the entertainment industry has also changed. While cable
and satellite networks are still very popular, consumers are now turning to live streaming video services such as Netflix and Hulu.
Over the past couple of years, mobile platforms have become the main way for people to watch live video content. In total, they spent
more than $2 billion on these services in 2014, according to the latest Deloitte survey.
